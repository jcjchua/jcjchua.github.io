"Coffee, tea, pastries, and furry critters."

A humble abode that unites dog-lovers and beautiful canines. We are a non-governmental organization inspired by Bau House in Seoul, South Korea. All dogs are fostered from our partnerships with local shelters and cared for by our employees and volunteers. Our goal is to help these dogs get adopted into forever-homes with the help of amazing customers, like you. Part of the proceeds not only go into helping run the cafe, but are also donated to our local shelters. So come join us, buy a cup of coffee and sit alongside a Siberian Husky or a Corgi, or spoil them with organic dog-treats.
- Walk the Dog Cafe
"Cafe and food area is separate from the dog playpen."

We care about the safety of our dogs and visitors. This is why our cafe and food area is separate from the dog playpen. Food and fur will not mix. We make sure to perform weekly inspections and adhere to the city safety standards. After all, it's the least we can do to make sure everyone has a good time.
